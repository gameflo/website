Workshops

We offer several engaging play-based workshops that were created in collaboration with teaching professionals and tied to Common Core standards for Mathematics. Almost all of the students who have gone through a workshop have expressed that they felt more confident working with data and solving problems. Contact us to learn more!

Probability

Our Probability workshop focuses on teaching students the principles of probability and expected value through the lens of shooting in basketball. Students will not only learn the basics and theory but will also gain practical experience making decisions using probability from various game-based activities and a fun deck-building card game.

Expressions & Equations

In our Expressions & Equations workshop, students will learn the power of mathematical modeling by breaking down popular measures of basketball player productivity and working together to create their own models. Students will gain conceptual knowledge of dependent and independent variables and apply algebraic expressions in an engaging simulation game.
