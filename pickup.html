About Pickup! Basketball

Get ready for Pickup! Basketball - the fast-paced deck builder game, that’s quick to learn and tough to master. Draft and build your roster from a league of 40 unique ballers, including floor generals who make their teammates better and lockdown defenders to neutralize your opponent. Pick your shots and compete in a strategic 3-on-3 match.

Join Us

Pickup! Basketball was born when we were trying to find ways to combine our love of basketball and card games. We’ve spent the past year playtesting and refining the game with various combinations of tabletop game players and basketball fans alike. We’re confident that the game will be fun for a wide variety of people and we’re ready to share it with the world.


We have used the game in workshops to help kids understand probability and basic statistics though their interest in basketball. We’re hoping the funds from the game will help us reach out and work with more K-12 STEM programs, camps, schools and afterschool programs. Kickstarter is just the first step in our journey.
